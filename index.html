How Phishing Attacks Work

1. Fake Email or Message

Attackers send emails or SMS messages pretending to be trusted brands like banks, Amazon, or PayPal. These messages create urgency to trick users into clicking malicious links.

2. Fake Login Page

Victims are redirected to fake websites that look identical to legitimate platforms. Once login credentials are entered, attackers steal sensitive information instantly.

3. Urgency & Fear Tactics

Messages often include threats like account suspension or prize rewards to force immediate action without careful verification.
